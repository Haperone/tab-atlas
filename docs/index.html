Tab Atlas

A calm new-tab dashboard that groups your open Chrome tabs by domain — searchable, savable, and organized into folders and workspaces.

  • See all open tabs across windows, grouped by domain
  • Search by title or URL, and spot duplicates at a glance
  • Save pages for later, file them into folders, restore from a searchable archive
  • Focus Sweep: review many tabs as one deliberate batch
  • Capture and restore whole workspaces
  • 100% local — no account, no server, no analytics
